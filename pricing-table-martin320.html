First we don't think any of the smartwatches are worth it at the moment. Many only have one day battery life and the functionality doesn't really give you much. A good wristband for sports and a nice normal watch will probably be a better option at the moment depending of the main use that you are going to give to your new toy ;)

See below the ones that we think are the most fashionable for your budget. The smartwatches have been around for a while but we think a lot more can be done in this space, they are not still good enough to spend €400 on them in our opinion. If you want a luxury smart watch then the price range starts at €1,200 and still we think the smart capabilities of the watch are not worth it.

If you are an iPhone user the obvious would be to go for the Apple watch but we don't particularly think is good looking and the apple watch 2 should be coming very soon, we would advice you to wait and see prices of this new apple watch as they might surprise us with a "cheap" version with most of the capabilities as they did with the iphone SE. Autonomy seems to be a big problem for apple at the moment in iphones and smartwatches so we don't recommend it unless they solve that :).
