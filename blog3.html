6 December 2015

Chromecast Audio
Cast your music to your Speakers

This gadget solves a problem that a lot of people have...and it's one of those that just works, simple and great!

If you have a good soundsystem at home, you probably don't want to buy a cheap bad quality bluethooth speaker to just stream the songs from your phone :)

Chromecast allows you to stream music through your speakers without cables, and not only that , it makes it sound so much better that when you play it connecting you phone to your speaker via the jack or bluetooth. Chromecast plays the music from the cloud not from the device, so no matter how bad your smartphone sound is...if you have good speakers, the music will still sound great on them.

When you Chromecast is connected you can still use your phone to get a phone call something that we couldn't do when we have our phone connected via the jack to our speakers. You can also allow casting from devices not signed into your Wi-Fi network via a PIN-based guest mode, to allow your guest choose the music and create great atmosphere for the best party ever :) !!

a few things that you have to consider:
- You don't have to connect your phone/tablet to your speaker with cables but you do have to plug the Chromecast into an outlet for power.

- It streams from the cloud so you can use it for soundcloud, spotify etc.. If you're using an app that doesn't have native Chromecast support, technically you can opt to mirror all of the audio coming out of your mobile device or your computer's Web browser and cast that to a speaker instead but we haven't tested this feature.

- At the moment you can not cast to multiple Chromecast audio but...this feature is coming!! and we are very excited about this. Full house sound system control at your fingertips with a few old hifi speakers.

It was exactly what we needed to make the house fully soooooound. We love it!
