YOUR MATCH (09/10/2015): Moov Now
First to let you know that none of this trackers are specifically precise when we are talking about less than 100€, but what they are good at are at telling you...move your ass ;)
Our recommendation is the Moov Now. The option 2 (Xiaomi) is actually what we will have recommended for somebody that has not used this type of devices before and a low budget. The price is AMAZING!! and it does the basics well, saying that, if you like what you see, you will probably want to upgrade soon to any of the other models.
We recommend Moov Now because has a pre-order offer that just cannot be missed and it is the only one focused on sports and with coach facility to really push you, the 7 minute work out will be our favorite :) .Plus you can use it on your ankle so you don't need to worry about how it looks like, its design is cool though. If you convince somebody to buy it with you the price os each would be around 65€ :)
Your option 3 is the Garmin vivofit 2 mainly because is one of the trackers that has display so you can see your progress without the phone and is one of those that tells you to move, so if you want to burn calories might be one to consider....a bit heavy though
