Hello Brite! and thanks for contacting us.

Your match is the Kindle Amazon. There are very few options on kid tablet on Ireland. Our recommendation will be the 7inch amazon kindle edition, is not HD like option 2 but it is a bit bigger and has micro SD slot which the 6 hasn't, 8GB is very little for a tablet. Problem with this one...Amazon does not deliver this item to Ireland (we think is because it offers 1 year Fire for Kids Unlimited and that is not available in Ireland). I'm not sure you have Parcel Motel but you can use it to deliver it to Ireland, if you don't have it takes very little to register and the price for one parcel is that little as €3.95 Parcel Motel Services and as Amazon delivers free to UK you will save the shipping cost. It is very easy to use, we can explain you how to use it if that is a problem.

We like as well the Robin 7' - 177€ but it seem to be out of stock in all the Dublin Argos shops. There are others sold in other countries but as far as Ireland goes...there doesn't seem to be anything better than the Amazon one, the others that we have seen don't look good enough to spend the money on them.

The other option is to buy a "grown up" tablet and add profiles for your child, and there are a few apps out there that will help you controlling the tablet (but that will need a bit of set up, not much but is not "out of the box"). You can ask us more about this option if you are thinking in going this route.
