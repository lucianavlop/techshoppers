{{ 'GADGETWEEK' | translate }} - New iPhone 7 Family - What is new?

￼

This week Apple showcased the new iPhones. So what is new? and how they compare with their older brothers?

Camera
iPhone 7 Plus has dual camera as the iPhone 6 Plus and maintains the 12 megapixels but now it has dual-lens that allow you to x2 zoom without loosing picture quality.
The lens are much faster. The iPhone 7 Plus has a new 6-element f/1.8 lens.Apple says 50% more light gets to the sensor this time around, which will improve low-light performance.
Also, it is possible to shoot in RAW mode to edit the photos with more precision on the phone or PC.

Selfie camera
The FaceTime camera on both iPhones got better as well.
7-megapixel front camera with f/2.2 aperture that has auto image stabilisation and does Full HD video recording
So in terms of cameras it does seem clear that iPhone 7 is definitely a step up

Processor and Memory
Better processor and 3GB of memory something that will probably not be noticed but most of the users, only if you do a lot of multitasking and complex task frequently

Extras
Water resistant, new mate black finish , a wider colour gamut screen but same resolution and new wireless earphones (which paradoxically imply the need of buying more cables/adapters). We are not sure anybody should change its iPhone 6 if we take into account these new features.

So basically....in our opinion, the camera is the best improvement of this new iPhone and only people that really likes mobile photography should upgrade to it :)
