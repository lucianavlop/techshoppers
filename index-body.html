What would you say if we tell you that you can alter the sound of the world around you? in the office, metro, concerts... that you can actually adjust the hearing to your liking.

These wireless bluetooth earbuds from Doppler labs are trying to give us a bit of a superhero hearing ability. We have been listening of VR (virtual reality) for a while but we never got options when we talk about the hearing sense.

These earbuds adjust, via an app, the volumes of particular sounds around you.
For example. Do you want to change the volume of the TV without afecting the rest of the people watching? Avoid the noise in a busy restaurant when you are having dinner with your girlfriend/boyfriend (that might not be a good scenario)? Or eliminate the noise in the metro or even the office, replacing it with white noise, so you can concentrate? These earbuds mean to do all these things. You can even play with the bass, mid-range and treble tones when you are in a concert or listening your favorite music.

As an idea it actually sounds amazing! An reviews have been giving praise to this innovative product that was available in Kikstarter for $199.

Today we are in a park writing and listening to the sound of the birds, and we are enjoying it, but we can think of a handful of situations where we would like to have these earbuds at hand...definitely a product to watch!!
