We know Dji for its drones but today we are going to talk about the DJI Osmo camera. Dji has brought all their drones work in camera stabilisation down to the ground and into your hands

Yes, there are better cameras, but we would struggle to get the quality of this one at the same price. With until 4k video recording it is one to watch

An attachment allows you to connect your smartphone to it, which enables you to use it as a monitor to view a live image (via Wi-Fi) from the camera

The microphone seems to be the biggest flaw but depending of what use you are going to give to the camera, it might not bother you that much.
